About Me

Craft, Precision
& Character

With over a decade behind the chair, I've built a reputation for cuts that don't just look good in the mirror — they hold their shape for weeks and work with your natural hair, not against it.

Every client who sits down gets my undivided attention. I listen first, then consult, then cut. No rushed appointments, no cookie-cutter results.

Based in downtown Brooklyn, my studio is a relaxed space where good music plays, the coffee's strong, and the results speak for themselves.
